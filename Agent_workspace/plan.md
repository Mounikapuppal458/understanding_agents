# Article Plan: Human Body and Its Architecture

1. **Introduction**: Define the human body as a complex, organized machine.
2. **Organization Levels**: Cells, Tissues, Organs, Systems.
3. **Major Systems**:
    - Skeletal (Support)
    - Muscular (Movement)
    - Nervous (Control/Communication)
    - Circulatory (Transport)
    - Respiratory (Gas Exchange)
    - Digestive (Energy/Nutrients)
4. **Integration**: How these systems work together in daily life.
5. **Practical Examples**: Breathing, running, eating.
6. **Maintenance**: Health tips.
7. **Conclusion**: Summary and appreciation for body complexity.
